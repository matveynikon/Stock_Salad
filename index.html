Market News Summarized

Get the Overall Sentiment Scores, Popularities and Most Relevant News Headlines for S&P 500 Stocks.
Intro
Reading news, reports and recommendations one by one can be tiring and can often give you an incomplete and subjective view of stocks, and getting mixed signals from different sources can make it hard to decide what stocks to buy, sell or trade as opposed to other stocks.



Here at Stock Salad, for Each Stock, You Get the Full Picture by Getting The:
  • Overall sentiment of the stock based on all news articles, reports and recommendations published in the last 5 days


  • Total number of news articles, reports and recommendations published in the last 5 days that mentioned that stock


  • News articles, reports and recommendations from the last 5 days sorted by relevance for that stock


  • Brief description of that stock/company

Gets the Sentiment, Mentions and Popularity of Top S&P 500 Stocks.
Gets the Sentiment, Mentions and Popularity of the Stocks You're Most Interested In.
Gets the Top Stocks by Sentiment and Their Mentions and Popularity.
Gets the Lowest Stocks by Sentiment and Their Mentions and Popularity.
